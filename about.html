About Sparkle Cleaning Solutions

Professional, friendly and detail-focused cleaning for homes and businesses.

Our Story

Sparkle Cleaning Solutions was founded in 2018 with a simple goal: to make clean, healthy spaces more accessible to everyday people. We started with two cleaners and a handful of clients in our local community.

Today, we serve homes, student apartments and offices across the region, still focused on personal service, quality work and fair pricing.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable cleaning services that create hygienic, refreshing spaces for homes and workplaces.

Vision

To become the region's most trusted modern cleaning brand through efficiency, innovation and service excellence.

Our Values

  • Realiability and punctuality
  • Respect for clients' property and privacy
  • Continuous improvement and training
  • Eco-friendly and safe cleaning practices
